Guacamole

Description

Simply a quick recipe for tasty guacamole! Great with tortilla chips or as a topping for Mexican foods!

Ingredients for Guacamole

  • 2 Avocados
  • 1 Small Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Ripe Tomato, Chopped
  • 1 Lime, Juiced
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Peel and mash avocados in a medium serving bowl. Chill for half an hour to blend flavors.
  2. Stir in onion, garlic, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Season with remaining lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill for half an hour to blend flavors.
